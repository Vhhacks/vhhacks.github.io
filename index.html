What is vhHacks

vhHacks is one of Greater Vancouver's only hackathons dedicated to high school students (like you!) More than one hundred students gather and collaborate to build and showcase a project in under 24 hours. No experience? No problem! vhHacks is beginner friendly, so anyone can join and learn a new skill!

Frequently Asked Questions

Have something on your mind? Check below!

Can I attend?

Of course! vhHacks is open to all high school students of all skill levels. Whether you've already been in many hackathons before, and you're pursuing computer science, or an absolute beginner, we welcome you all! Artists, musicians, athletes, gamers, everyone! If you're older (post-secondary or above) and have experience in computer science, apply to be a mentor!

How much does it cost?

Nothing! vhHacks is completely free of charge at the event. However, if you are booking a stay near the event, or taking public transport, those will cost money.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is what you make of it! All participants are encouraged to use the 24 hours to solve a problem using technology, whether that's making the solution itself, marketing it through a website, or investigating new approaches. We are also a learning environment, and an opportunity to try and learn new things! Haven't touched website design before? Try your hand at HTML. Want to learn how to use an arduino? Try some new hardware! No experience is required to attend.

Will there be food?

Yes. Food and snacks are provided throughout the event, even overnight! (We know you night owls get the munchies) There are also restaurants around the venue to buy food and snacks, and you can always bring your own!
