What is vhHacks?

vhHacks is Greater Vancouver's first free hackathon dedicated to high school students. More than one hundred students will gather and collaborate to build and showcase a project in 48 hours. No experience? No problem! vhHacks is beginner friendly, so anyone can join to learn, build, and hack.

Pick a path!

vhHacks is all about learning! If you haven't attended a hackathon before, pick a path below and dive into the world of hacking! More info to come!

Designer

Design the user interface for your hack.

Programmer

Write the code that makes your hack work.

Game developer

Create a unique game in a short period of time.

Hardware hacker

Use hardware to solve modern problems.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon. Students come together in teams to build cool software and hardware projects over the course of 48 hours.

Who can attend?

vhHacks is open to all high school students of all skill levels. If you're older (post-secondary or above) and have experience in computer science, you can apply to be a mentor.

How much does it cost?

Nothing! vhHacks is completely free of charge.

What if I'm a beginner?

Hackathons are very beginner friendly. Whether you're an absolute beginner, a new computer science student, or an experienced hacker, you're the right fit for vhHacks. Artists, musicians, athletes, gamers, everyone is welcome!

Where is the event?

vhHacks 2021 will be taking place online on Devpost and Discord. You can find a link to the Devpost here an invite to the Discord server here.

Teams

How big can my team be?

Teams are usually 2-4 people in size. Teams bigger than 4 will be split up into smaller ones, since larger teams aren't usually the best solution for a 24 hour event.

What happens if I don't have a team?

No worries! A lot of other hackers are in the same position. vhHacks staff will help you find other teammates and provide you with opportunities to find others!
