What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a place where you and hundreds of other people learn, build, and create new technologies
over the course of one weekend! Hackathons let you try learning a new skill, commit to that
crazy idea you've never had time for, or make new friendships and strengthen old ones.

Who can attend?

vhHacks is open primarily to, and structured for, high school or secondary students.
However, if you're in middle school, or university, you are welcome to apply as well
