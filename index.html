FAQ

  • What is a Hackathon?

    Diamonds are made under pressure, and so are innovations. A Hackathon is a design event where teams of people work together to build a project in a short time. Think of a it like a science fair or a case competition, but you only have a day to prepare.

  • Who is this for?

    While Hackathons can be for anyone, this particular event is for students 18 and younger, and requires no experience. You can still registor to be a mentor or volunteer if you're older though.

  • What does it cost?

    Nothing! Just register beforehand so we know you will be coming.

  • What should I bring?

    Government or School-issued photo ID; This (link) waiver signed by your parent or guardian(s); A laptop, charger and headphones.

  • What if I don't have a team?

    There will be team-forming and team-building events at the beginning of the Hackathon. We'll make sure to match you with somebody.
