COVID-19

vhHacks update on the current situation

As you’ve no doubt been following, it’s become increasingly clear over the last week that we must all do our part to help slow the COVID-19 pandemic in our communities. Authorities have been advising cancellation of large gatherings, and after a difficult discussion with the team, we’ve made the difficult decision tonight to postpone vhHacks 2020 indefinitely, to help you and our community stay safe. We don’t know exactly when the new date will be yet, but the vhHacks team is working hard to make sure that, when the time is right, the hackathon can take place as planned for all of you, and we’ll be sure to keep you updated as soon as we know more.

Sincerely,

The vhHacks Team.
